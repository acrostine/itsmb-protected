Service Availability & Continuity Management (SACM) (context) ()
Service Availability & Continuity Management (SACM) executes service availability and continuity plans at the Execute level, ensuring reliable service delivery and resilience. It progresses through evaluation, identification, maintenance, and implementation phases, from evaluating service availability and continuity to maintaining and implementing service availability and continuity plans. It receives SLAs for availability requirements from Service Level Management (SLM) to identify service availability and continuity requirements. It produces change requests for availability for Change Management (CHM), enabling effective management of service changes.
