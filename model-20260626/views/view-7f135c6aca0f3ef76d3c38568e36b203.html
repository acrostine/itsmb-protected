Maintain and implement information security controls and policies - Flow ()
A request for a security audit triggers the process of Maintain and implement information security controls and policies, initiated by the Information Security Management (ISM) team. This results in the development and refinement of security controls and policies that are then delivered to the relevant stakeholders within the organization. In general terms, this process encompasses the identification, implementation, and ongoing management of security measures designed to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information. It follows a lifecycle that includes assessment, implementation, monitoring, and adjustment of security policies, with decisions made based on risk assessments and compliance requirements. Stakeholders involved typically include IT personnel, compliance officers, and management teams who rely on the effectiveness of these controls to safeguard organizational assets. This process exists to ensure that the organization maintains a robust security posture, which is vital for protecting sensitive information and maintaining trust with clients and partners. Without it, the organization would be vulnerable to data breaches and compliance failures, leading to potential financial and reputational damage. The primary output of this process is the refined security controls and policies, which are delivered to consuming parties within the organization. PROCESS STEPS 1. [Monitor control effectiveness] Explanation: Information Security Analyst monitors the implemented controls using dashboards to assess if they are effective; if effective, proceed to policy delivery; if ineffective, initiate adjustment. Role: Information Security Analyst Application Service: Monitoring Dashboard Application: Monitoring Tool 9. [Deliver refined policies] Explanation: ISM communicates and delivers the refined security controls and policies to stakeholders (IT personnel, compliance officers, management teams) for ongoing use. Role: Information Security Manager (ISM) Application Service: Policy Distribution Tool Application: Document Management 10. [Adjust controls and policies] Explanation: Information Security Analyst modifies the controls and policies based on monitoring feedback, then loops back to generate a new change request for re-implementation.
