Continual Service Improvement Management (CSI) (context) ()
Continual Service Improvement Management (CSI) executes control objects at the Execute level, ensuring enhanced service quality and efficiency. It progresses through evaluation, planning, implementation, and review phases, from managing evaluation of improvements to managing implementation of improvements. It receives customer feedback improvement suggestions from Customer Relationship Management (CRM) to identify potential enhancements. It produces change requests for improvements for Change Management (CHM), enabling the systematic implementation of service enhancements.
