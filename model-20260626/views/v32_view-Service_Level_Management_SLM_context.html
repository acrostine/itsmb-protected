Service Level Management (SLM) (context) ()
Service Level Management (SLM) executes service level agreements at the Execute level, ensuring effective management of service quality and performance. It progresses through the phases of maintaining SLAs, maintaining service catalogues, and maintaining supporting agreements, from initial setup to ongoing management. It receives customer SLA requirements from Customer Relationship Management (CRM) to align services with customer expectations. It produces SLAs for reporting requirements for Service Reporting Management (SRM), enabling informed decision-making and performance tracking.
