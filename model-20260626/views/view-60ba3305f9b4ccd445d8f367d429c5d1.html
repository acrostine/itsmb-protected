Identify reporting requirements - Flow ()
A request from a senior management team to assess the performance of a newly implemented service triggers the Identify reporting requirements process within Service Reporting Management (SRM), resulting in a detailed report specification that outlines the necessary data and metrics to be collected. This process defines the specific reporting needs related to services and processes, ensuring that the relevant information is accurately captured and communicated to stakeholders. It operates within the broader context of Service Reporting Management, making critical decisions about what data is required, how it should be formatted, and who will receive the reports, thereby facilitating effective service oversight and performance evaluation. The Identify reporting requirements process exists to ensure that the organization can make informed decisions based on accurate and relevant service data. Without this process, the organization would struggle to monitor service performance effectively, leading to potential inefficiencies and missed opportunities for improvement. The primary output, the report specification, is delivered to the consuming parties who rely on this information for strategic decision-making. PROCESS STEPS 1.
