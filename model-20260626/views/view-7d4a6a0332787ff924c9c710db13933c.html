Manage demand and service proposals - Flow ()
A request for a new service proposal triggers the Manage demand and service proposals process, initiated by the Service Portfolio Management (SPM) team, resulting in a comprehensive service proposal document delivered to the relevant stakeholders. This document outlines the demand for the service and the proposed solutions to meet that demand. The Manage demand and service proposals process operates within the Service Portfolio Management capability, focusing on assessing and prioritizing service demands while ensuring alignment with organizational goals. It encompasses the evaluation of service proposals throughout their lifecycle, facilitating informed decision-making by engaging various stakeholders involved in service delivery and management. This process exists to ensure that the organization effectively responds to market demands and optimizes its service offerings, thereby enhancing customer satisfaction and operational efficiency. Without this process, the organization risks misalignment between service offerings and customer needs, leading to potential revenue loss and decreased competitiveness. The primary output, the service proposal document, is delivered to consuming parties who rely on it for strategic decision-making. PROCESS STEPS 1.
