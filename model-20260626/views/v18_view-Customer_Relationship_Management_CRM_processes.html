Customer Relationship Management (CRM) (processes) ()
Customer Relationship Management (CRM) executes customer interactions at the Execute level, ensuring enhanced customer satisfaction and loyalty. It progresses through the phases of handling customer complaints, maintaining the customer database, managing customer satisfaction, and performing customer service reviews, from initiation to completion. It receives SLAs and reports for reviews from Service Level Management (SLM) to inform service performance assessments. It produces customer requirements for services for Service Portfolio Management (SPM), enabling alignment of services with customer expectations.
