Maintain report specifications - Flow ()
A request for a new service performance report triggers the Maintain report specifications process, initiated by a Service Reporting Management (SRM) analyst who defines the necessary parameters and formats for the report. The resulting artefact is a detailed report specification that outlines the data requirements and delivery timelines for the report. In general terms, the Maintain report specifications process ensures that reports on services and processes are accurately specified, produced, and delivered to meet the needs of stakeholders. This process encompasses the lifecycle of report creation from initial request through to final delivery, making critical decisions about data inclusion and presentation. It operates within the Service Reporting Management (SRM) capability, engaging various stakeholders who rely on these reports for informed decision-making. This process exists to provide clarity and consistency in reporting, delivering value by ensuring that stakeholders receive timely and relevant information. Without it, the organization would face challenges in tracking service performance and compliance, potentially leading to uninformed decisions and inefficiencies.
