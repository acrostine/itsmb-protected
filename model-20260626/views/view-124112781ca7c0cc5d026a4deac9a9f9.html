Maintain the service portfolio - Flow ()
A request for an update to the service portfolio triggers the Maintain the service portfolio process, initiated by a Service Portfolio Manager who assesses the current offerings and identifies necessary adjustments. The result is a refined service portfolio that aligns with organizational goals and stakeholder needs. This process encompasses the ongoing management of services throughout their lifecycle, ensuring that the service portfolio remains relevant and effective. It involves evaluating existing services, incorporating new offerings, and retiring outdated ones, with decisions made collaboratively among stakeholders such as service owners and business leaders. The Maintain the service portfolio process is integral to the Service Portfolio Management (SPM) capability, which aims to optimize service delivery and alignment with business strategy. The existence of this process is crucial for maintaining an agile and responsive service portfolio that meets evolving market demands and customer expectations. Without it, the organization risks misalignment between services and business objectives, leading to inefficiencies and lost opportunities. The primary output of this process is the updated service portfolio, delivered to consuming parties who rely on it for informed decision-making. PROCESS STEPS 1.
