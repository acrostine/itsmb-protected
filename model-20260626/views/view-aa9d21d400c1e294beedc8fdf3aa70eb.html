Handle customer complaints - Flow ()
A customer initiates the process of Handle customer complaints after experiencing an issue with a service, prompting the Customer Relationship Management (CRM) team to investigate and resolve the matter. The outcome of this process is a resolution report that details the actions taken and the final decision communicated to the customer. In general terms, the Handle customer complaints process aims to address and resolve customer grievances, ensuring that their concerns are acknowledged and managed effectively. This process operates within the Customer Relationship Management (CRM) capability, facilitating the lifecycle of complaint handling from receipt to resolution, involving various stakeholders such as customer service representatives and management. The purpose of this process is to enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty by effectively managing complaints, which, if neglected, could lead to customer dissatisfaction and potential loss of business.
