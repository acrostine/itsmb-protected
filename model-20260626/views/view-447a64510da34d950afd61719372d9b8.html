Evaluate service performance - Flow ()
A request for a performance review is initiated by the Capacity Management team, triggering the Evaluate service performance process, which results in detailed performance reports delivered to relevant stakeholders. This ensures that the service performance aligns with established targets and expectations. The Evaluate service performance process assesses the effectiveness and efficiency of services within the Capacity Management (CAPM) capability. It involves analyzing performance data, making decisions regarding service adjustments, and engaging with stakeholders to ensure that service levels are maintained or improved. This process is critical for managing the lifecycle of service capacity and performance, ensuring that the organization can meet its operational goals. This process exists to provide insights into service performance, enabling proactive management of capacity and ensuring that service targets are met. Without it, the organization risks underperformance, potential service outages, and customer dissatisfaction.
