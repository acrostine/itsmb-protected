Problem Management (PM) (context) ()
Problem Management (PM) executes problem resolution at the Execute level, ensuring the effective handling and resolution of service-related issues. It progresses through identification, investigation, resolution, and maintenance phases, from identifying problems to maintaining the KEDB. It receives CMDB for problem investigation from Configuration Management (CONFM) to support thorough analysis and resolution. It produces change requests to resolve problems for Change Management (CHM), enabling the implementation of necessary changes to improve service quality.
