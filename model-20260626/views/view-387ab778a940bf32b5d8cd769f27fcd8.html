Maintain the customer database - Flow ()
A request to update customer contact information triggers the Maintain the customer database process, initiated by a customer service representative. The result of this process is an updated customer database that reflects the latest information for accurate service delivery. In general terms, the Maintain the customer database process ensures that customer information is current and accessible, supporting the broader Customer Relationship Management (CRM) capability. This process involves regular updates, validation of data accuracy, and decisions regarding data retention, engaging various stakeholders such as customer service teams and data management personnel. This process exists to enhance the quality of customer interactions and service delivery, ultimately fostering stronger relationships with customers. Without it, the organization risks operating with outdated information, leading to potential service failures and customer dissatisfaction.
