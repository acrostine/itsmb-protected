Change Management (CHM) (processes) ()
Change Management (CHM) executes change evaluation and approval at the Execute level, ensuring effective management of changes within the organization. It progresses through the phases of change evaluation, approval, implementation, and review, from initial assessment to final review. It receives change requests for capacity from Capacity Management (CAPM) to assess and prioritize necessary changes. It produces change information for CMDB for Configuration Management (CONFM), enabling accurate tracking of changes in the system.
