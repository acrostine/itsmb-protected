Capacity Management (CAPM) (context) ()
Capacity Management (CAPM) executes control over service capacity at the Execute level, ensuring optimal service performance and resource utilization. It progresses through Evaluate service performance, Identify service capacity and performance requirements, and Maintain and implement capacity plans, from initiation to completion. It receives SLAs for capacity requirements from Service Level Management (SLM) to define the necessary capacity standards. It produces Change requests for capacity for Change Management (CHM), enabling effective resource adjustments.
