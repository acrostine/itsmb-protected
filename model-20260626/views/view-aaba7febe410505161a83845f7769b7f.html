Monitor supplier performance - Flow ()
A scheduled review within Supplier Relationship Management (SUPPM) triggers the Monitor supplier performance process, initiated by the Supplier Relationship Manager who assesses the performance metrics of key suppliers. The artefact resulting from this process includes a comprehensive performance report that highlights areas of compliance and potential improvement. In general terms, the Monitor supplier performance process evaluates supplier effectiveness against established criteria, facilitating informed decision-making regarding supplier relationships. It encompasses the entire lifecycle of performance monitoring, including data collection, analysis, and reporting, ensuring that both internal and external stakeholders are engaged in the evaluation process. This process is integral to the Supplier Relationship Management capability, which aims to foster healthy supplier partnerships and optimize performance outcomes. This process exists to provide critical insights that enhance supplier collaboration and drive operational efficiency within the organization. Without it, the organization risks misalignment with supplier capabilities, leading to potential disruptions in service delivery and quality. The primary output, the performance report, is delivered to relevant stakeholders for further action and decision-making. PROCESS STEPS 1.
