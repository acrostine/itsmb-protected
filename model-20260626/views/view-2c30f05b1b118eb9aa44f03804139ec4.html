Manage change implementation and review ()
A request for a system upgrade triggers the Manage change implementation and review process, initiated by the Change Management team, resulting in a comprehensive report detailing the implementation outcomes and any necessary adjustments. This report is then shared with relevant stakeholders to ensure alignment and transparency. In general terms, the Manage change implementation and review process oversees the planning, approval, and assessment of changes, ensuring that they are executed in a controlled manner to mitigate any adverse impacts on services. This process involves evaluating the change requests, coordinating with stakeholders, and reviewing the outcomes to ensure compliance with the established Change Management capability. This process exists to deliver structured oversight and accountability in change initiatives, which is crucial for maintaining service integrity and operational stability. Without it, organizations risk uncoordinated changes that could disrupt services or lead to inefficiencies.
