Manage evaluation of improvements - Flow ()
A request for a service enhancement triggers the Manage evaluation of improvements process, initiated by the Continual Service Improvement Management (CSI) team, resulting in a comprehensive report detailing potential improvements and their anticipated impacts. This report is then delivered to relevant stakeholders for further action and decision-making. The Manage evaluation of improvements process systematically assesses proposed enhancements to services and processes, ensuring that they align with organizational goals. It follows a lifecycle of planning, implementation, and review, making critical decisions about which improvements to pursue based on their feasibility and expected value. Stakeholders involved in this process include service managers, process owners, and other relevant parties who contribute to the evaluation and decision-making. This process exists to ensure that the organization continually refines its services and processes, thereby enhancing efficiency and customer satisfaction. Without it, the organization risks stagnation and may fail to respond effectively to changing needs, leading to missed opportunities for improvement.
