Supplier Relationship Management (SUPPM) (context) ()
Supplier Relationship Management (SUPPM) executes control of supplier relationships at the Execute level, ensuring effective management of supplier performance and information. It progresses through phases of database maintenance, performance monitoring, evaluation, and reporting, from maintaining the supplier database to monitoring supplier performance. It receives OLAs and UAs for supplier evaluation and supplier information for new suppliers from Service Level Management (SLM) and Service Portfolio Management (SPM) to support supplier assessment and onboarding. It produces supplier information for delivery to Service Portfolio Management (SPM) and supplier information for agreements to Service Level Management (SLM), enabling informed decision-making and compliance with service agreements.
