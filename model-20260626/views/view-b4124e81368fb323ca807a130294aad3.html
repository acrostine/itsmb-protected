Manage customer satisfaction - Flow ()
A customer satisfaction survey is initiated by the Customer Relationship Management (CRM) team following a scheduled event, such as the completion of a service interaction, resulting in a comprehensive report on customer feedback. This report is then delivered to relevant stakeholders for further analysis and action. The Manage customer satisfaction process is designed to gather, analyze, and respond to customer feedback to enhance service delivery and maintain strong relationships with customers. It operates within the Customer Relationship Management (CRM) capability, ensuring that the organization remains attuned to customer needs and expectations throughout the customer lifecycle. Key decisions include identifying areas for improvement and determining appropriate responses to customer feedback, involving various stakeholders such as service teams and management. This process exists to ensure that customer voices are heard and acted upon, ultimately driving customer loyalty and satisfaction. Without it, the organization risks losing touch with customer needs, leading to potential dissatisfaction and attrition. The primary output, the customer feedback report, is delivered to consuming parties who utilize the insights to inform strategic decisions and service enhancements. PROCESS STEPS 1.
