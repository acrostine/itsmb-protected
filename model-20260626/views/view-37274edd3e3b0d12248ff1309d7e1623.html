Manage services through their lifecycle - Flow ()
A request for a new service enhancement triggers the Manage services through their lifecycle process, initiated by the Service Portfolio Management (SPM) team, resulting in updated service documentation and a revised service offering. This ensures that the service remains aligned with business needs and stakeholder expectations. In general terms, this process oversees the entire lifecycle of services within the Service Portfolio Management capability, making decisions on service initiation, modification, and retirement based on performance metrics and market demands. It involves various stakeholders, including service owners and business units, to ensure that services are effectively managed and aligned with organizational goals. The purpose of this process is to deliver value by ensuring that services are continuously optimized and relevant, ultimately enhancing customer satisfaction and operational efficiency. Without this process, the organization risks service obsolescence and misalignment with business objectives, leading to potential loss of competitive advantage.
