Manage workflows needed to resolve incidents and fulfil service requests - Flow ()
When a user reports a system outage, the process of Manage workflows needed to resolve incidents and fulfil service requests is triggered within Incident & Service Request Management (ISRM). The IT support team initiates this process, leading to the creation of a resolution plan that addresses the incident and outlines steps for fulfilling any related service requests. This process encompasses the coordination of tasks and resources necessary to restore agreed service operations following an incident while also addressing user service requests. It follows a structured lifecycle that includes assessment, prioritization, and resolution, making critical decisions about resource allocation and timelines. Stakeholders involved include IT support staff, service managers, and end-users who rely on timely incident resolution and service fulfillment. The existence of this process is vital for maintaining operational efficiency and user satisfaction within the organization. Without it, incidents could lead to prolonged service disruptions, negatively impacting productivity and user trust. The primary output of this process is the resolution plan, which is delivered to the consuming parties, ensuring that their needs are met promptly and effectively. PROCESS STEPS 1.
