Maintain service catalogues - Flow ()
A request from a business unit to update the service offerings triggers the Maintain service catalogues process, which is initiated by the Service Level Management (SLM) team. The result of this process is an updated service catalogue that reflects the current agreements on service quality with customers and suppliers. The Maintain service catalogues process is responsible for ensuring that service catalogues are accurate and up-to-date, facilitating the definition and evaluation of service quality agreements. This process involves reviewing existing services, incorporating feedback from stakeholders, and making necessary adjustments to align with organizational goals. It operates within the broader Service Level Management (SLM) capability, which encompasses the management of service quality and performance. This process exists to provide clarity and transparency regarding the services offered, ultimately enhancing customer satisfaction and trust. Without it, inconsistencies in service offerings could lead to misunderstandings and degraded service quality, negatively impacting relationships with customers and suppliers. The primary output of this process is the updated service catalogue, which is delivered to consuming parties for their reference and use. PROCESS STEPS 1.
