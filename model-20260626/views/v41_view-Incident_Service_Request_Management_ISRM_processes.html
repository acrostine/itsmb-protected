Incident & Service Request Management (ISRM) (processes) ()
Incident & Service Request Management (ISRM) executes workflows needed to resolve incidents and fulfill service requests at the Execute level, ensuring efficient incident resolution and service delivery. It progresses through the phases of incident identification, incident resolution, service request fulfillment, and closure, from managing incidents and service requests to managing major incidents. It receives CMDB for incident resolution from Configuration Management (CONFM) to aid in accurate incident handling. It produces incident trend information for Problem Management (PM), enabling proactive problem management and continuous service improvement.
