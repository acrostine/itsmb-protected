Manage change evaluation and approval - Flow ()
A request for a significant software update triggers the Manage change evaluation and approval process, initiated by the Change Management team. The outcome of this process is a formal approval or rejection of the change, documented and communicated to relevant stakeholders. This process evaluates proposed changes to ensure they align with organizational goals and do not negatively impact existing services. It encompasses the assessment of risks, benefits, and resource requirements, facilitating informed decision-making among stakeholders involved in the Change Management capability. The purpose of this process is to maintain service integrity and minimize disruptions by ensuring that all changes are thoroughly evaluated before implementation. Without this process, the organization would face increased risks of service outages and inefficiencies.
