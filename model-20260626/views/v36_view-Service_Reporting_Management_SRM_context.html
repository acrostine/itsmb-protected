Service Reporting Management (SRM) (context) ()
Service Reporting Management (SRM) executes reporting requirements at the Execute level, ensuring informed decision-making and service improvement. It progresses through identifying reporting requirements, maintaining report specifications, and monitoring the production and distribution of reports, from initiation to completion. It receives performance and utilisation data from Capacity Management (CAPM), service availability data from Service Availability & Continuity Management (SACM), and SLAs for reporting requirements from Service Level Management (SLM) to define reporting needs. It produces service reports for improvement for Continual Service Improvement Management (CSI) and reports for customer management to Customer Relationship Management (CRM), enabling enhanced service delivery and customer satisfaction.
