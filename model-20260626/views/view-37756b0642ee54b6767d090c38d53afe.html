Monitor the production and distribution of reports - Flow ()
When a scheduled event occurs within Service Reporting Management (SRM), the process to Monitor the production and distribution of reports is initiated, ensuring that the necessary reports are generated and distributed to the relevant stakeholders. This results in timely and accurate outputs that inform decision-making across the organization. In general terms, this process oversees the entire lifecycle of report production and distribution, making critical decisions about the types of reports needed and their delivery to consuming parties. It operates within the Service Reporting Management capability, which is focused on specifying reports related to services and processes, ensuring that stakeholders receive the information they require for effective operations. This process exists to provide essential insights into service performance and operational efficiency, delivering value by enabling informed decision-making and accountability. Without it, the organization would struggle with inconsistent reporting and a lack of visibility into service operations, ultimately undermining performance. The primary output of this process is the reports produced, which are delivered to the consuming parties. PROCESS STEPS 1. Role: Automation Application Service: Report Generator Application: Reporting Tool 7. [Review report accuracy] Explanation: SRM Manager reviews the generated report for accuracy and completeness; may approve or request revisions. Role: SRM Manager Application Service: Report Review Dashboard Application: ITSM Platform 8. [Revise report content] Explanation: The system revises the report based on feedback from the SRM Manager, returning to generation step. Role: Automation Application Service: Report Generator Application: Reporting Tool 9. [Distribute approved reports] Explanation: The system distributes service reports for improvement to CSI and reports for customer management to CRM. Role: Automation Application Service: Distribution Service Application: ITSM Platform 10. [Confirm receipt] Explanation: SRM Manager confirms receipt of reports by CSI and CRM; if not confirmed, requests redistribution.
