Service Portfolio Management (SPM) (context) ()
Service Portfolio Management (SPM) executes service portfolio management at the Execute level, ensuring effective management of service offerings and their lifecycle. It progresses through the phases of maintaining the service portfolio, managing demand and service proposals, and managing services through their lifecycle, from initiation to retirement. It receives customer requirements for services from Customer Relationship Management (CRM) to align service offerings with customer needs. It produces change requests for new services for Change Management (CHM), enabling the introduction of new services that meet market demands.
