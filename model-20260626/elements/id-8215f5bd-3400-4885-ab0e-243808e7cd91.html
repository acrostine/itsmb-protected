Change Management (CHM)

Change requests for improvements are produced by Manage evaluation of improvements and Manage implementation of improvements within Continual Service Improvement Management (CSI) and passed to Change Management (CHM) when improvements are identified, enabling Manage change evaluation and approval and Manage change implementation and review. It is consumed by Manage change evaluation and approval to facilitate the assessment and approval of changes.
